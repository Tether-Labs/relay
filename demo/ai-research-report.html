Executive summary

We gave GPT-4o-mini access to a sales assistant inbox and measured how often outbound emails contained PII leaks, wrong recipients, or policy violations.

Key finding: Supervision cuts leaks by ~64%, but 1 in 6 still slip through. Enforcement at the decision boundary drives residual leaks to zero — at a 17.5% intervention rate.
